Frequently Asked Questions

What is mental health awareness?

Mental health awareness involves recognizing the importance of mental well-being and reducing stigma around mental health issues.

How can I manage stress effectively?

Some ways to manage stress include practicing mindfulness, exercising, and seeking professional help if needed.

What should I do if I feel overwhelmed?

Reach out to a trusted friend, family member, or a therapist to talk about your feelings and seek support.

Where can I find mental health resources?

You can explore platforms like Psychology Today for verified therapists and resources.

How can I help a friend struggling with mental health?

Be a good listener, offer your support, and encourage them to seek professional help.
